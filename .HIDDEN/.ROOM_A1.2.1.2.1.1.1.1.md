The stage is not a vacuum; it has viscosity. All signals traversing the stage are subject to:

## Latency

The temporal delay between an Instigator's action and the Receiver's observation.

## Degradation

The entropic loss of signal clarity (noise injection) proportional to the distance between agents.

## Question
